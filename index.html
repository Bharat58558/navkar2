MS FLANGES

Flanges are pipe fittings that are used to connect pipes or fittings together to perform different functions. They have a raised part so that they could be attached well to the other component either by welding or by bolting. Mild Steel Flanges are common in usage as they provide a medium range of strength and corrosion resistance in a balance. There are also different functionalities of the flanges we make. Our MS blind flange is used in applications that require a closure of pipes.
